Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This is a plain-English privacy policy. Current Collective is a small coaching practice — not a large corporation. This page explains honestly and simply how your information is handled.

Who we are

Current Collective is a one-to-one coaching practice run by Koda. You can contact us at kodaeddy04@gmail.com or through the contact form at withcurrentcollective.com.

What information we collect

We only collect information you choose to share with us. This happens when you fill in the contact form on our website. The information collected includes:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Any message or personal detail you include in your message

We do not use analytics tools, advertising trackers, or any third-party data collection on this website.

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to respond to your enquiry. We will not:

  • Add you to a mailing list without your explicit consent
  • Share your information with any third party
  • Use your information for marketing purposes

How your message is sent

The contact form uses a service called FormSubmit (formsubmit.co) to forward your message to our email inbox. FormSubmit does not store your message after forwarding — it is delivered directly to our Gmail inbox and nowhere else.

How long we keep your information

Messages received via the contact form are stored in our email inbox. We aim to delete enquiries that do not lead to an ongoing coaching relationship within a reasonable time after responding. If you become a client, information is retained only for as long as necessary and in accordance with professional practice standards.

Confidentiality

Everything you share through the contact form is treated with strict confidentiality. We take the privacy of anyone reaching out seriously, particularly given the personal nature of coaching and therapy work.

Cookies

This website does not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or tracking of any kind. Your browser may set strictly necessary session cookies as part of normal website operation — these contain no personal information and are deleted when you close your browser.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR and the Australian Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at kodaeddy04@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

If we make any significant changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to check back occasionally.

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please get in touch at kodaeddy04@gmail.com.
